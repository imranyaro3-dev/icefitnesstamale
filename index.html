// What We Offer

OUR SERVICES

State-of-the-art equipment and expert coaching across every discipline — designed to help you reach your peak.

🏋️
WEIGHT TRAINING

Full free-weight section with Olympic barbells, dumbbells up to 100kg, power racks, and dedicated bench press stations. Guided programs for all levels.

🏃
CARDIO ZONE

40+ machines including treadmills, ellipticals, rowing machines, and assault bikes. Individual screens and heart-rate monitoring built in.

🥊
COMBAT & BOXING

Dedicated boxing ring, heavy bags, speed bags, and an open mat area for martial arts. Classes run daily at 6AM, 12PM, and 6PM.

🧘
YOGA & RECOVERY

Dedicated studio with morning yoga, meditation, foam rolling, and stretching programs led by certified instructors. Open to all members.

🔥
GROUP CLASSES

HIIT, Spin, Zumba, Pilates, and more — up to 18 group classes weekly. All included in your membership. No extra fees, no reservations needed.

🧑‍💼
PERSONAL TRAINING

One-on-one sessions with certified trainers who craft custom plans around your goals — whether that's fat loss, muscle gain, or athletic performance.

🥤
NUTRITION BAR

In-house smoothie bar and meal prep station. Post-workout protein shakes, fresh juices, and healthy snacks. Nutrition consultations also available.

❄️
ICE RECOVERY

Our signature cold plunge pool, contrast therapy, and sauna suites. Accelerate recovery and reduce inflammation after every session.
